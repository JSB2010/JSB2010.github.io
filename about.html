About Me

Born with a rare genetic condition called Albinism, my vision has never been very good. In elementary school, I needed an iPad to see the screen/board, and this need for technology turned into a hobby.

Interests

App Development

I like creating apps and websites that can help me or others, and learning new languages and styles of programming.

Public Transportation

Because of my vision, I am unable to drive, so I take public transit as much as possible. I like to do research on our current systems, and how to improve them.

Finance/Economics

I was fortunate enough to be able to open an account at Young Americans Center for Financial Education and since then, I have been an advocate for childhood financial education, and I am now on the Youth Advisory Board of the center.

Philanthropy/Volunteering

Through my middle school, I participated in a class called Eagle Fund.
